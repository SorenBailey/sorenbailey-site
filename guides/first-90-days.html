The First 90 Days Roadmap

A focused plan for your first real estate move.

Thesis

Focus beats everything in year one. One submarket. One strategy. Four relationships. A hundred underwriting reps. Then your first offer.

Days 1-30 — Study / Submarket / Strategy

  • Free material first. No paid courses in month one.
  • Baseline vocabulary: ARV, LTV, DSCR, seasoning, cap rate, points, origination.
  • Pick ONE submarket. Drive it weekly.
  • Pick ONE strategy. Commit for 12 months.

Days 30-60 — Relationships

  • Investor-friendly agent
  • Two lenders (community/credit union + hard money/bridge)
  • Contractor (referral only, small test job first)
  • One off-market source (wholesaler, investor, agent)

Days 60-90 — Underwriting Reps

  • 3-5 properties per day, full underwriting
  • Use the Beginner Underwriting Checklist
  • Track patterns, refine your buy box
  • First offer written by day 90 when a real deal shows up

What To Skip

  • Expensive courses
  • Multi-market scanning
  • Strategy-hopping
  • Gurus
  • Entity gymnastics for deal zero
  • "Big" first deals

Mindset That Compounds

Show up for the boring work. Be honest about what you don't know. Protect downside first. Reputation compounds faster than the balance sheet.
