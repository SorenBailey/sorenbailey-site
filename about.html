About

Operator-led real estate investing education from a young operator buying through BRRRR, distressed assets, and disciplined underwriting.

Who this is

I'm a 24-year-old investor based in Illinois. I've bought 85+ units, mostly distressed. I'm a top 3% real estate agent primarily through my own transactions. I don't buy at the top of the market — I buy what other people are walking past.

Why this exists

Most real estate content online is either aspirational or predatory. The operators who are actually doing deals are usually too busy to teach, and the people who teach loudly often haven't done the reps. I wanted a place that was different — clean, practical, and useful for both new and active investors.

What you'll find here

  • Long-form YouTube breakdowns on BRRRR, distressed, underwriting, flips, and wholesaling
  • Free printable guides you can use on real walkthroughs
  • Lessons from actual deals — what worked, what didn't, what I'd do differently
  • A calm operator voice, not a guru voice

How I think about teaching

Everything here is framed as "this is what I've done and how I think about it." Your market, your lender, your timeline, and your risk tolerance may be different. I try to be explicit about Illinois-specific examples versus general U.S. principles.

What I won't do

  • Income flex content
  • "Anyone can do this fast" claims
  • Predatory wholesaling advice
  • Guaranteed-outcome language
  • Coaching upsells disguised as free content
